our story

Currently in Hanna, just behind the terrace, you’ll find our latest concept: Teppanyaki. It means ‘cooked or roasted on a grill’ in Japanese.

Teppanyaki is a modern form of Japanese cuisine, with a variety of dishes prepared in front of you, on a display of technique, aromas and flavors.

It’s a unique dinner experience, consisting on an eight course menu, in which every detail is taken care of, to offer our guests the quality they deserve.

We are eager to show you this unique proposal that will surely please all your senses.

meet our chef

Born in the picturesque city of Kyoto, Japan, Hiroshi Tanaka discovered his passion for cooking at an early age. Inspired by his family's culinary traditions, Hiroshi began his training at the prestigious Tsuji Culinary Institute, where he honed his skills in sushi preparation and kaiseki cooking.

With over 20 years of experience, Chef Hiroshi Tanaka is known for his meticulous attention to detail and commitment to excellence. His restaurant has received numerous awards and accolades, making it a must-visit destination for Japanese food lovers in Costa Rica.
